What is a Crop Season?

A Crop Season tracks one complete growing cycle of a crop from planting to harvest.

Use Crop Seasons to:
  • Track which crops were planted in which fields each year
  • Record yield and quality for performance analysis
  • Monitor crop rotation and soil health
  • Compare varieties and identify best performers
  • Keep historical records of field productivity
Workflow:
  1. Add Season: Create record when you plan/plant
  2. During Growing: Track maintenance and issues
  3. At Harvest: Record yield, quality, and notes
  4. Analysis: Compare performance across fields/years
